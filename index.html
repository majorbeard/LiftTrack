Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is LiftTrack?

    LiftTrack is an all-in-one fitness tracking solution. With it, you can track your weight lifting, nutrition, and sleep. You can also connect with registered local food vendors, friends, share your progress, and join fitness challenges. We’ve got everything you need to get started.

  • How does the tracker work?

    Our workout tracker allows you to log exercises, sets, and reps, and automatically adjusts your program for progressive overload to ensure continuous improvement.

  • Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. No long-term commitments.

  • How do I order meals from vendors?

    You can browse registered vendors in your area through our Nutrition Tracker and place orders directly from the app. Meals can be customized based on your dietary needs.

  • Is my data secure?

    Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your personal and fitness data is kept safe and private.
