Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the workout tracker work?
  • Our workout tracker allows you to log exercises, sets, and reps, and automatically adjusts your program for progressive overload to ensure continuous improvement.
  • Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
  • Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. No long-term commitments.
  • How do I order meals from vendors?
  • You can browse registered vendors in your area through our Meal Vendors list and place orders directly from the app. Meals can be customized based on your dietary needs.
  • Is my data secure?
  • Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your personal and fitness data is kept safe and private.
