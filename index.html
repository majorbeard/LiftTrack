Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is LiftTrack?

    LiftTrack is an all-in-one fitness tracking solution. With it, you can track your weight lifting, nutrition, and sleep. You can also connect with friends, share your progress, and join fitness challenges. We’ve got everything you need to get started.

  • How does the tracker work?

    Our workout tracker allows you to log exercises, sets, and reps, and adjust your program for progressive overload to ensure continuous improvement.

  • Is my data secure?

    Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your personal and fitness data is kept safe and private.
